Digital Supper

An informal supper evening in which people gather to share food and discuss the digital revolution in the broadest sense – from a billion profiles on facebook to a smartphone in every pocket.

Their purpose is to empower more people inside and outside the "digital world" to understand and shape the impact of the digital revolution on all our lives.

About Digital Supper
 

Mixing It Up

Each supper has a roughly even mix of guests from inside and outside the digital world in order to maximise the opportunity for learning and the exchange of ideas.

Inviting

Rather than the host inviting everyone, half the guests get to invite someone. This creates a richer mix and gives more people an active role in the event.

Fun and Food

Suppers take place at someone's home or a special venue rather than a restaurant. The makes the supper more informal, participatory and fun.

Bring Questions

These evenings are about exploration and welcoming discussion. Come with questions not answers and be open to exploring new topics and areas.
